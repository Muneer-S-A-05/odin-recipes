Sarbath Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Nannari Syrup
  • 2 tablespoons Lemon juice
  • 1 cup Chill Water
  • Ice cubes

Instructions

  1. To the serving glass add few ice cubes along with 3 tablespoon nannari syrup.
  2. Add lemon juice from ½ lemon.
  3. Add 1 cup water.
  4. Mix it well and serve chilled!
